Mental health has emerged as a significant concern in our modern era, particularly affecting college students at an alarming rate. To combat this pressing issue, our team, WaspSwatterz, proposes an innovative solution: Rakshak.

Rakshak is an all-in-one tool designed to support real-time mental health and wellness monitoring, providing immediate therapeutic feedback or alerts based on the user's vocal expressions. Our platform aims to pinpoint areas requiring attention and offer tailored remedies to individuals in need.

We have built a chatbot whom you can talk to via voice and share your problems. He will then help you by suggesting you remedies. We also provide you the access to various world class psychologists in case you feel that our chatbot alone can't help you.
